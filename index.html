AMAR SADANAND TECH PARK

Amar Sadanand Tech Park developed by Amar Builders is a dominant name on Baner road, Pune, with key commercial developments. This Grade A estate provides in-house parking and has more free parking options nearby. Easy accessibility to public transportation and its closeness to Pune railway station is an asset to the property. The property offers spacious and skillfully designed commercial office space also well equipped with modern amenities to support business needs. In addition to this, the landholding has some celebrated hotels and fine dining restaurants like The Orchid, Holiday Inn, Post 91, Incognito etc. nearby. The location is 20 km from Pune Airport.
